Yoga Poses Chart

Yoga Pose Description
Downward Facing Dog A foundational yoga pose that stretches the entire body, particularly the back, hamstrings, and shoulders.
Tree Pose (Vrikshasana) A balancing pose that strengthens the legs and improves focus and concentration.
Warrior II Pose (Virabhadrasana II) An energizing pose that builds strength in the legs and arms while opening the hips and chest.
Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana) A gentle backbend that stretches the spine, chest, and shoulders, while strengthening the back muscles.
